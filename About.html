About Us

What is Rooted WoodWorks

Welcome to Rooted Woodworks, LLC, Northern Michigan's Premiere Custom Finish Carpentry. We are devoted to providing you with exceptional quality, craftsmanship, and personalized detail in every job and every cut we make. Whether you need Custom Wood Work, Crown Moulding or Custom Wood Door Installation, we are here to make your project our priority.

We approach each Custom Wood Work project with our core values in mind: attention to detail, quality, professionalism, and overall consistency. Please look at our photo gallery or contact us to see how we can bring our service to your home.


What Makes Us Unique

Fine Finish Carpentry is like icing on a cake and it is what not only unifies a design but sets it apart from ordinary. Rooted Woodworks specializes in installing finish trim and making and installing various types of one of a kind accents to your home. Baseboards, crown molding, wainscoting, chair rail, wall panels, beadboard, and siding are all types of finish carpentry we can add to your project to make it distinctive. We are experts at adding these finishing touches to your project to ensure pleasing proportions, clean lines, flawless miters, trim lines that carry through from windows to doors to cabinets, perfectly fitted hardware and other well-crafted details that will make your house transform into the home you only dreamed of.
